Why I Love Playing Basketball

I love playing basketball because it allows me the opportunity to be engaged intellectually and physically. The game brings such satisfying and enjoyable movements and reactions physically as I move around the court, passing, dribbling, and shooting. But it also brings adaptive, reactive, and anticipatory thoughts and choices on offense and defense. This is where the intellectual engagement and knowledge of the game come in, which I enjoy as equally as the physical.

My Top 3 Skills in Basketball

Each of these skills combines physical ability with mental decision-making.

  1. Passing
    • Reading defenders and finding open teammates
    • Fast decision making in transition
    • Creating passing lanes with ball fakes
  2. Shooting
    • Consistent shooting form and release
    • Knowing when to pull up versus driving
    • Adjusting from different locations on the court
  3. Dribbling
    • Protecting the ball under pressure
    • Changing speed and direction
    • Using combinations to create space
